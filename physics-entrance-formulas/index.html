Energy Transfer/Waves

    • f = 1÷T
    • T = 1÷f
  • v = fλ
  • i = r (in reflection of light)
  • In refraction of light: (Snell's Law)
    • n = (sin i)÷(sin r)
    • n1 sin i = n2 sin r
  • For light waves:
    • v = c÷n
    • n = c÷v

Motion

  • Δanything = Finalanything - Initialanything
  • a = (v-u)÷t
    Newton's Equations of Motion were derived from that equation:
    1. v = u + at
    2. s = ut + (1÷2)at2
    3. v2 = u2 + 2as
  • Fnet = ma (Newton's Second Law of Motion)
  • v = s÷t (in objects with constant velocity)

Electricity

    • I = Q÷T
    • Q = It
  • V÷I = R
  • In series circuit:
    • R = R1 + R2 + R3 + ...
    • V = V1 + V2 + V3 + ...
  • In parallel circuit:
    • 1÷R = 1÷R1 + 1÷R2 + 1÷R3 + ...
    • I = I1 + I2 + I3 + ...

Energy

  • KE = (1÷2)mv2
  • WD = ΔKE = (1÷2)mv2 - (1÷2)mu2
  • CR = (vb - va)÷(ua - ub)
  • GPE = mgh
